Copy these two files to the Pico filesystem:

  Pico_Firmware\main.py
  Pico_Firmware\pico_i2c_bridge.py

The easiest method is Thonny:
  1. Plug in the Pico.
  2. Open Thonny and select MicroPython (Raspberry Pi Pico).
  3. Open main.py and save it to the Pico.
  4. Open pico_i2c_bridge.py and save it to the Pico.
  5. Reboot the Pico.

When the Pico boots, it should present a USB serial port and respond to:
  PING
with:
  OK
